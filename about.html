About Minnuz Foods

Bringing You the Authentic Taste of Kerala

Fresh, high-quality batters crafted for perfect Idlis, Dosas, Appams, and more.

At Minnuz Foods, we believe great food starts with great ingredients. Our fresh, preservative-free batters are made with the finest grains to ensure the most authentic flavors, just like homemade.

We are committed to quality, freshness, and convenience, making cooking easy while keeping the tradition alive. Whether it’s a crispy dosa or soft idli, our batters help you prepare delicious meals effortlessly.

Our Story

Years of Tradition & Quality

Since

1999

Founded with a passion for authentic South Indian flavors, Minnuz Foods has been delivering fresh, high-quality batters to homes for over two decades. We take pride in using the finest ingredients, ensuring our customers enjoy delicious, homemade-style Idlis, Dosas, and Appams without the hassle of preparation.

Our journey began with a simple mission – to make traditional cooking easier while preserving its rich taste. Today, Minnuz Foods stands as a trusted name, bringing the essence of Kerala’s culinary heritage straight to your kitchen.
